Grab Some Friends and Drive

Is your school not provided a bus? We’ll be providing gas reimbursements for drivers who drive at least two other people. Make sure that your friends also RSVP and select “Driving”.

On Saturday February 20th, you will be asked to submit your gas receipts, so please make sure you hold onto those! The total cap for carpooling is $150.

For the return trip, you will need to mail in your second gas receipt to the following address:

Siebel Center for Computer Science
HackIllinois
201 N. Goodwin Ave
Urbana, IL 61802

Free parking will be providing in lot B22 - right next to Siebel Center for Computer Science.
